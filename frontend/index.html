AI-Powered Rural Symptom Checker with Telemedicine Routing

A 'virtual first-aid station' for rural India

Helping rural patients understand symptoms, predict possible conditions, and connect to the nearest available doctors - all without needing to travel first.

The Problem

  • Limited Access: 1 doctor per 10,000+ people in many villages
  • Delayed Diagnosis: People travel long distances for minor issues
  • Misinformation: WhatsApp forwards replace medical advice
  • Emergency Confusion: Don't know where to go in critical situations

Our Solution

  • AI Symptom Checker - Understands what might be wrong
  • Doctor Connector - Finds nearby help
  • Health Guide - Provides verified information
  • Emergency Assistant - Guides in critical situations

Emergency Protocol

Red Flag System: Critical Symptoms Detected

If you experience any of these symptoms, seek immediate medical attention:

  • Severe chest pain or pressure
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Sudden confusion or difficulty speaking
  • Severe uncontrolled bleeding
  • Loss of consciousness
